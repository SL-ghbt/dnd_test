# The Drunken Raven

**Summary:** A tavern located in the Shadow Quarter. This is the primary meeting spot where Vaelin receives his mission assignments from his handler, Talia.

## Notable NPCs
* **Tarren:** The tavern bouncer. A male human who is stoic and laconic, but friendly to Vaelin.
