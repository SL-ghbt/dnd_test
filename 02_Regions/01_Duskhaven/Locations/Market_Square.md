# Market Square

**Summary:** The commercial and trade hub located in the exact center of Duskhaven.

## Notable NPCs & Vendors
* **Mira "Quickfingers":** A female halfling and independent fence who operates out of a market stall. She has ties to the Nightshade Guild.
* **Garrick Stonehand:** A male dwarf merchant with a cluttered shop. He is anxious but tries to appear cordial. He struggles to manage his debts to various criminal organizations.
* **Silas Blackwood:** A middle-aged male human who operates "Blackwood's Baubles". He operates as a jeweler, but also deals in illicit goods.
* **Grizzel Cogsworth:** A meticulous male gnome who is obsessed with intricate mechanisms and peculiar artifacts. He operates "The Cog and Candle" - Arcane Curios & Clockwork Emporium, where he houses a large collection of arcane and clockwork marvels.
