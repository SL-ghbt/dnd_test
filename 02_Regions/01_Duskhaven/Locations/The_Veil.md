# The Veil

**Summary:** A labyrinthine district with narrow, twisting streets and a distinct noir aesthetic. 

## Notable Establishments & NPCs
* **The Shaded Lantern:** A magic shop owned by Elaris, an ancient and wise female high elf sage with a detached curiosity. She attended the college of Alistair Finchley. She is assisted by Lyra, a female human.
* **The Crimson Coin:** An elite gambling den operated by Sterling Van, a well-dressed and charismatic male human.
