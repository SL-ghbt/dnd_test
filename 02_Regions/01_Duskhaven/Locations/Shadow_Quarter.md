# Shadow Quarter

**Summary:** The poor and neglected area of the city.

## Notable Establishments & Turfs
* **The Drunken Raven:** A tavern where Vaelin receives his mission assignments from his handler, Talia.
* **The Nest (Cogsworth Tenement):** An abandoned apartment building known as the Cogsworth Tenement. The upper floor serves as the headquarters for The Ratlings.
