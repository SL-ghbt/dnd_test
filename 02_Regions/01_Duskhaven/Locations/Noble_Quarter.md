# Noble Quarter

**Summary:** Located in the northwest, this district is the home of old money and the high-born of Duskhaven. 

## Notable NPCs & Locations
* **Lord Varyn:** A corrupt noble who is suave and charming, living in one of the estates.
* **Silas "Inkfinger":** A middle-aged male gnome who operates a bookstore here. He is secretly a forger and alchemist, and maintains a collegial relationship with The Forger from the Harbor Syndicate.
