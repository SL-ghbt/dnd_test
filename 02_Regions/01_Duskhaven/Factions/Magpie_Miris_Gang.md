# Magpie Miri's Gang

**Summary:** Magpie Miri's gang is a small-time street gang operating in Duskhaven. 

## Gang Members
* **Magpie Miri (Leader):** A female human. She wears a signature magpie feather in her hair. She is arrogant and not as smart as she thinks she is.
* **Grol:** A male human who acts as a bully.
* **Pip:** A male goblin who is cowardly.
